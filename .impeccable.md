## Design Context

### Users
Salon / spa owners checking business performance daily — typically on a phone between clients or at end of day. They need fast clarity on revenue, bookings, and marketing ROI.

### Brand Personality
Friendly · Modern · Energetic — approachable but data-driven. Should feel like a smart assistant, not a spreadsheet.

### Aesthetic Direction
Mobile-first (375px phone frame). Light theme. Purple-led brand (#5f2eea) with accent teal and green for positive signals. Clean, tight spacing. Data needs to be readable at a glance.

### Design Principles
1. Numbers first — the data is the hero, chrome is secondary
2. Show the story — visualize trends and drop-offs, don't just list values
3. Energy through color — use brand purple and green purposefully to signal good news
4. Friendly density — mobile means compact, but never cramped
5. No AI-slop patterns — no side stripes, no gradient text, no generic shadow cards
